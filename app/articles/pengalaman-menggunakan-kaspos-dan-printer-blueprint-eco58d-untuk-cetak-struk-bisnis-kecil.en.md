A while back, I was on the hunt for a simple cashier app I could use for a small business — nothing complicated, no mandatory login, no internet connection required, and most importantly, something that could print receipts. After trying out a few options, I stumbled upon **KasPOS** on the Play Store. What caught my eye? It's clean, lightweight, and there's no monthly subscription. Exactly what I needed.

## Starting with the Free Version

KasPOS has a free tier you can use right away without registering. The catch: you're limited to a **maximum of 10 product items**. For initial testing and getting a feel for the app, that's more than enough. The interface is clean too — no ads getting in the way — so I could really focus on trying out the core features.

## Upgrading to Premium: Only Rp35,000

Once I felt confident it was the right fit, I went ahead and bought the premium version. The price is very reasonable — **Rp35,000 as a one-time purchase** (taxes not included). No recurring fees, and all features unlock immediately. For me, that's a great deal, especially for an offline app that's genuinely designed to make day-to-day operations easier.

## Pairing It with the Blueprint ECO58D Printer

I'm using a **Blueprint ECO58D** thermal printer, and the Bluetooth pairing process went **surprisingly smoothly**. Receipts printed directly from KasPOS without any complicated setup. Even the **automatic cash drawer opening** feature works when the printer is connected via an RJ11 cable.

## Features That Kept Me Hooked

A few other features that made me confident KasPOS is a long-term solution for me:

- **Backup & Restore**  
  Super easy to back up your data and restore it when you switch phones or reinstall the app.

- **Receipt Layout Settings**  
  You can customize how your receipts look. There are two options:

  - **Standard receipt**: full-detail layout, great for stores that need comprehensive information on every receipt.
  - **Compact receipt (paper-saving mode)**: a more condensed layout, ideal for conserving thermal paper.

- **Automatic Cash Drawer Opening**  
  If your printer supports RJ11, KasPOS can automatically open the cash drawer right after printing a receipt.

- **No Internet Required**  
  Completely offline. Perfect for selling at outdoor events or anywhere with spotty connectivity.

## Final Thoughts

If you're looking for a lightweight, no-fuss cashier app that prints receipts and works with thermal printers like the **Blueprint ECO58D**, **KasPOS is absolutely worth trying out**.  
Personally, I'm really happy with how it performs. It's affordable, feature-packed, and works entirely offline. The only minor downside is the 10-item cap on the free version, but the upgrade is so cheap it's hardly a dealbreaker.
